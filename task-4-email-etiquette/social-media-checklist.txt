Social Media Do's and Don'ts

DO's:
1. Share useful and positive content.
2. Respect others' opinions.
3. Use privacy settings properly.
4. Think before posting.
5. Maintain a professional profile.

DON'Ts:
1. Do not share personal information publicly.
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
3. Do not engage in online arguments.
4. Avoid spreading fake news.
5. Do not misuse someone else's content.
